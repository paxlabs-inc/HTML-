How do I get PAX?

Swap in from USDC or major assets inside Matrix itself, or fund through Paxport. Builder accounts can auto-top-up from a connected balance.

What counts as an action?

One verb in an executed plan. Multi-verb plans sum their steps; the simulation diff shows the exact price before you approve.

When does the free tier reset?

Every 24 hours, 10 PAX, automatically. Unused allowance doesn't roll over.

Do failed plans cost anything?

No. Plans that fail simulation are discarded unsigned and unbilled. You pay only for intents that execute.

Can rates change under me?

Rates are versioned on-chain. A new table ships as a new version with notice; in-flight scheduled intents keep the version they were created under.
