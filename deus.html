How does a zero take rate sustain the network?

Deus is infrastructure for the Paxeer economy, not a profit center. The network earns from chain-level activity that services generate — settlement, scheduling, streams — so the marketplace itself doesn't need a cut.

How are disputes handled?

From the voucher record. Both parties signed the quote and the delivery; disagreements resolve against that bilateral evidence rather than a platform's say-so.

What does listing require?

A ServiceRecord with declared verbs, pricing, and at least one supported settlement flow, signed by a Paxport DID. Attestation history accrues from real usage — there's no pay-to-rank.
