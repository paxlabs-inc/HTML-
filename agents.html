SKILL.mtx

Capabilities are declared, not discovered

Every agent ships a SKILL.mtx manifest: the verbs it may use, the tools it may touch, the limits it operates under. Sessions scope down from there — never up.

Identity

AgentWallets on the fee lane

Agents transact from dedicated wallets on Paxeer's agent-native payment lane, authenticated with ed25519 DIDs through Paxport.

Billing

Metered like everything else

Agent actions price from the same on-chain rate card as human intents. Budgets are guardrails, enforced at the simulation gate.

Audit

Every step on the record

Plans, simulations, signatures, receipts — the full loop is logged and replayable. Autonomy you can depose.

Self-improvement

Agents that get better, with receipts

The OODA loop — self-map, self-diagnose, self-optimize, self-verify — runs against measured outcomes, not vibes. Improvements ship only when verification passes.
